SEARCH and SORT

 

Adjacency

Multiple words entered together are searched as individual words.

To search for a phrase, enter the phrase in quotation marks. If no match is found, remove the quotation marks to search as individual words.

Examples:

  • planet jupiter will be searched as planet AND jupiter; results will contain both words but not necessarily adjacent
  • "Cold war" results will show records that contain the words Cold war, as a phrase (adjacent to each other)
Boolean operators

To search multiple words not as a phrase use:

  • AND to find all of the words in any order
  • OR to find any of the words
  • AND NOT to exclude one or more words

When using more than one operator, use parentheses to group words.

To search operators as words, enter them in quotation marks.

Synonyms: & replaces AND ; | replaces OR ; &| replaces AND NOT

Examples:

  • law and youth
  • polar and not bear
  • ("capital punishment" or "death penalty") and Canada
  • art and (mexico and not new)
  • War "and" peace, or "War and peace"
  • (art | painting) & (mexico &| new)
Proximity operators

Refine your search by using:

  • NEAR to specify words within 10 words of each other, in any order,
  • WITHIN # to specify words which occur within a set range of each other,
  • BEFORE to specify that a word must precede another,
  • AFTER to indicate that a word must follow another.

To search operators as words, enter them in quotation marks.

Synonym: ~ replaces NEAR

 

Examples:

  • women near men
  • canada within 11 peace-keeping operations
  • sunday before school
  • "Near" east, or "Near east"
  • "Before" time began, or "Before time began"
Truncation or Wildcarding

Words may be internally or right-hand truncated by using:

  • ? for one character within a word,
  • * (single asterisk) for any number of characters,
  • NOTE: truncation or wildcarding can only be used after at least two characters.

The words in the keyword synonym list have variant spellings and will be retrieved together under either spelling. To bypass this enter the word in quotes, e.g. "labour"

Examples:

  • wom*n and child*
  • czech*
  • ontario near econom*
  • teen* and "mass media"
  • Sven Anders*n
  • centre - will retrieve both centre and center
Common words and initial articles

All words, including one and two letter words and initial articles, are indexed and should be included in a search.

Initial articles (a, an, the, le, la, les) are ignored only if they are the first word in a search, but will be included if enclosed in quotation marks,

 

Examples:

  • Gone with the wind
  • "Le Blanc" or "Le" Blanc
  • b flat
  • "a" flat, or "a flat"
Field specifiers

Specify fields to search, using the following field abbreviations:

  • a: (for author field),
  • t: (for title field),
  • s: (for subject heading field)
  • n: (for notes field)

Multiple words will only be searched as a phrase. To search as words repeat the field abbreviation.

Examples:

  • t: Greenhouse gas and a: Canada
  • a: shakespeare and t: macbeth and recording
  • a: canada and a: industry and s: indust**
  • a: asimov and s: astronomy

 

LIMITING A KEYWORD SEARCH

SEARCHING TIP: To retrieve all records that meet a limit criteria, enter ** in the keyword box, pick or enter a limit and click on Search.

 
Language

Choose the language of publication, multilingual works are included:

  • ANY (default)
  • English
  • French
  • German
  • Italian
  • Spanish
  • Russian

To search by a language that is not one of the above, check here to find the appropriate three letter language code. Then enter the term lang followed by the three letter language code, e.g. langtur (for turkish), as the keyword you wish to search.
Material Type

Choose the type of material:

  • ANY (default)
  • Computer File - CD-ROM titles, excludes Web Resources,
  • Print Map
  • Books/Serials
  • Printed Music - Scores,
  • Film/Video, etc. - includes Videorecordings, videocassettes
  • Sound Recording - Music CD sound recordings
  • Spoken Recording - Speech sound recordings
Location

Location from the bibliographic record. Note that when there are multiple copies with different locations, such as a newspaper that we also have a copy in microfiche, these titles may not be included.

  • ANY (default)
  • ARC&RESEARCH COLLECTIONS - Includes rare books, special poetry collection and more (raf,rar,arc,spc)
  • AUDIO VISUAL - DVD, Videocassette(videorecording) CD (cdm,itr,itv,vid)
  • DATA CENTRE - part of MADGIC ( Maps, Data and Government Information Centre) (mdc)
  • FOLIO - Oversize (fol)
  • GOVERNMENT DOCUMENTS (ddf,ddm,ddn,ddr,ddv,mdw)
  • JOURNALS (in SER) - Print journals in the SER location sequence (ser)
  • MADGIC REFERENCE - Maps, Data and Government Information Reference (mde,mdr,mrr)

  • MICROFORM - Microfiche, microfilm (mcr,mfi,mfl)

Year: After and Before

Enter the publication range of dates. When entering a range of dates, the years entered will be EXCLUDED from the search. E.g. to search for 2000 to 2010, you must enter "After 1999 and Before 2011"

For a single date, enter the same year for Before and After.
Search and Sort

Three sort options are available:

  • Relevance (default) : Relevancy of a record is indicated by a one to five scale display. Ranking occurs on the fields that include the search term. The title field is most relevant, followed by author, subject and notes. All records with search terms in the same field are ranked according to the position of those terms and a variety of other factors. More information
  • Title : alphabetical (using a system algorithm)
  • Date : sorts in reverse order of date of publication, i.e. latest date first

A search that is entered in "quotes", can only be sorted by either date or title.

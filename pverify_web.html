Notice: Phishing Attempt

Many people at Carleton have received an email with the subject of ‘Library Account’ that appears to be from Carleton University. Do not click on the link in the message and do not reply to it. This is a phishing email and should be deleted immediately. More information about this phishing attempt.

If you have already clicked on the link or have already responded to this email, please change your password immediately.

Type the Library of Congress Subject Heading and press ENTER or click on SEARCH. If you are unsure of the exact Library of Congress Subject Heading, try a KEYWORD search.

Examples:

  • canada--history--1663-1713 (new france)
  • indians of north america--canada
  • social service--canada
  • shakespeare, william, 1564-1616

Subject Heading Search

Search Library of Congress or government document subject headings.

Library of Congress Subject Headings

Library of Congress Subject headings are assigned to the majority of items in the library's collection. Try these searches:

  • canada--history--1663-1713 (new france)
  • indians of north america--canada
  • shakespeare, william, 1564-1616 v

If you don't know which Library of Congress subject heading to search for, try a keyword search.

Government Document Subject Headings

Search subject headings assigned to government documents located in the Maps, Data and Government Information Centre (MADGIC).

Search Tips for Government Documents

Think of a keyword or phrase that describes your subject:

  • foreign relations
  • midwives

Add a geographical name. This can be a country, province/state, city, region, political grouping, etc.:

  • foreign relations developing countries
  • midwives ontario

Census and historical material should be searched by place:

  • Canada census 1991 census tracts ottawa-hull (cma)
  • Great Britian history 1898-1914 documents

Names of programmes, organizations and people can also be used as search phrases:

  • canada pension plan
  • world bank
  • fraser report
